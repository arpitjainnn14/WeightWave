About WeightWave

The Story Behind WeightWave

Once upon a time, there was a developer who loved coding and fitness. 🏃‍♂️💻

During a particularly challenging fitness journey, they found themselves juggling between multiple apps and spreadsheets to track their weight progress. The existing apps were either too complex with unnecessary features or too simple without the insights they needed.

One day, while watching their weight fluctuate on a basic line graph, they had an epiphany: "Why not create something that's both beautiful and practical?" And thus, WeightWave was born - a labor of love that combines elegant design with powerful insights.

The goal was simple: create a weight tracking application that's:

  • 🎯 Easy to use but powerful enough for serious tracking
  • 📊 Visually appealing with animated, insightful graphs
  • 📱 Accessible anywhere, on any device
  • 💡 Focused on what matters - your progress

I hope WeightWave helps people track their fitness journeys with style and simplicity. Whether they’re training for a marathon, working towards a healthier lifestyle, or simply keeping an eye on their weight, I hope the app makes their journey a little more enjoyable and rewarding.

Why Choose WeightWave?

📈 Smart Tracking

Beautiful animated graphs that make tracking your progress a joy. Color-coded trends help you visualize your journey at a glance.

🎯 Goal Setting

Set your target weight and watch your progress with a clear visual indicator of your goals.

📱 Always Accessible

Use WeightWave on any device - your progress is always at your fingertips.
